Just go to: https://account.onfire.net/merge Then you're prompted to create a new semi-account (the account is not usable on its own until at least one account is merged). When finished you are prompted to enter creditentials of the accounts you want to merge (you may need to complete a challenge). When finished your accounts are merged. BUT PAY ATTENTION: If you sign in to any of your old account within 3 months after merging, the merging process will be cancelled and you have to start again
