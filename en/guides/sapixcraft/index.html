How to install Sapixcraft

Yes, there is a reason I wrote this guide.

-1. Why I wrote this guide?

On the Sapixcord there are a few questions beeing asked on a daily basis:


  • How do I download the (texture) resource pack?
  • Whenever I try to download I get on a page where...
  • How does that (name here) installer work and what has it to do with Sapixcraft?

That lead me to writing this guide.

0. Introduction

  • Why does anyone use AdFocus, AdFly & co. ?
    Creating and offering resource packages costs money. Starting with the fees for the programs up to the costs for the webhosting. The creators don't want to sit on these costs, and among us, of course, gain a small profit. Websites such as AdFocus and the like pay for the user to stay on the site for a certain time and display advertisements. Since the providers cannot check each advertisement individually, it can also happen that insecure or intrusive advertising is displayed.
  • Are AdFocus and co. insecure?
    Of course not! You just have to follow my instructions carefully and not download everything you are prompted to.

1. Choose your download

Go to the official website and head over to the downloads section, select your branch, version and resolution. You will be redirected to AdFocus.

Dont click on "Download" buttons inside adverts

Below you can see a typical advert that has a large "Download" button. Users may think that this is the download they want but it is not. There are (for me) three ways to recognize an ad-download-button:

  • They don't match Design
    As you can see the advertisement doesn't quite match the design of the webpage (mine is dark and luaxblue)
  • They have nothing to do with the topic
    If you can read you're at a clear advantage! Read the text next to the download button, it is about Grammarly (a software to improve grammer, maybe I should give it a try). Therefore this is likely not to be your download.
  • They may be declared or have symbols on them
    On some pages you can see a line stating "Advertisement" above or near to adverts. In case of Sapixcraft, you can see a blue cross and triangle in the top-right corner, this is an indicator for Google ads.

2. Wait

On AdFocus you have to wait for a moment as you can read in the top-right corner where it says "Please wait x seconds". I've painted the screenshot below green and red. The green area is safe and you may click on buttons there whereas the red area is unsafe and you should keep you fingers away from it.
After the countdown expired, press the skip button. This redirects you back to the sapixcraft official site. If a new tab opens with anything other, just close that tab and press skip again.



In addition you may receive pop-ups saying that read like "x wants to send you notifications". I recommend you to always deny them.


3. Download

You did it! Just click the "Download Sapixcraft" button in the center of the screen.
Do not download anything other than a ZIP file


It did not work

Make sure you read each step carefully and try it again. If it still doesn't work, you may ask on the Sapixcord now.
